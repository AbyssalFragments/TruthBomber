# TruthBomber Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 16, 2026

## 1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains what information TruthBomber ("Bot") may collect, how it is used, and your rights regarding that information.

## 2. Information We Collect

The Bot may process the following information as required for operation:

### Discord Information

- Discord user IDs
- Discord server IDs
- Discord channel IDs
- Discord message metadata necessary to execute commands

### Technical Information

- Error logs
- Diagnostic information
- Command usage statistics
- Service performance metrics

## 3. Information We Do Not Intentionally Collect

Unless explicitly required by a feature, we do not intentionally collect:

- Passwords
- Payment information
- Real names
- Physical addresses
- Government-issued identification
- Private Discord messages

## 4. How Information Is Used

Information may be used to:

- Operate Bot features
- Process commands
- Prevent abuse and spam
- Debug errors
- Improve reliability and performance
- Maintain security

## 5. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information.

Information may be shared only:

- When required by law
- To comply with legal obligations
- To protect the security or integrity of the service
- With service providers necessary for hosting and operation

## 6. Data Retention

Information is retained only for as long as reasonably necessary to operate the Bot, investigate abuse, maintain security, or comply with legal obligations.

Logs may be periodically deleted or anonymized.

## 7. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

## 8. Third-Party Services

TruthBomber operates through Discord and may rely on third-party infrastructure providers.

Use of those services is governed by their respective privacy policies and terms.

### Discord

https://discord.com/privacy

## 9. Children's Privacy

The Bot is not intended for individuals who are prohibited from using Discord under Discord's Terms of Service.

## 10. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights regarding access, correction, deletion, or restriction of personal information.

Requests may be submitted through the project's support channels.

## 11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Continued use of the Bot after updates become effective constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

## 12. Contact

Questions regarding this Privacy Policy may be directed through the project's issue tracker or official support channels.
